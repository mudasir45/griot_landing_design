# Griot Academy Homepage Content

## Page Purpose

The homepage should quickly explain what Griot Academy is, who it serves, what programs are available, and what parents should do next. A parent should understand the offer in the first few seconds: structured K-8 enrichment in chess, coding, robotics, math, and AI literacy, with live classes, guided practice, and parent-visible progress.

## Target Audience

Primary audience: parents and guardians of K-8 students looking for organized enrichment outside the regular school day.

Secondary audiences: homeschool families, school leaders, after-school coordinators, and enrichment decision-makers looking for chess, coding, robotics, math, AI literacy, or STEM programming.

## Conversion Goal

Primary conversion: Book a Trial Class.

Secondary conversion: Join Founding Families.

Support conversions: View Programs, Get Class Updates, Request School Demo.
