# Griot Academy Programs Overview Content

## Page Purpose

The Programs Overview page helps parents compare Griot Academy programs and choose a clear starting point for their child. It should explain what each program is, who it is best for, what students practice, and what action parents should take next.

This page should not include weekly curriculum, full course details, or internal planning notes. It should be a parent-facing decision page.

## Target Audience

Primary audience: parents and guardians of K-8 students comparing enrichment options in chess, coding, robotics, math, and AI literacy.

Secondary audience: homeschool families, school leaders, after-school coordinators, and group organizers looking for structured enrichment programs.

## Conversion Goal

Primary conversion: Book a Trial Class.

Secondary conversion: Join Founding Families.

Support conversions: View a program, join a waitlist, get class updates, request a school demo, or contact Griot Academy.
